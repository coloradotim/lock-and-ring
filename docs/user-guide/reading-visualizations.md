# Reading Visualizations

Visualizations are supporting evidence, not the main rehearsal answer.

- Spectrum shows where acoustic energy is concentrated right now.
- Spectrogram shows energy over frequency and time.
- Metric labels and trends are the app's interpretation.
- Stable peaks over time often support clearer lock and ring.
- Raw colors and peaks can be affected by room sound, vowel shape, volume, and
  microphone placement.

Use visual detail when you want to inspect why a metric might have changed.
Use Signal Status and What Just Happened during active singing.
