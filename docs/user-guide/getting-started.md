# Getting Started

1. Choose the microphone you want to use.
2. Check the signal status at the top of Live Rehearsal.
3. Sing a sustained chord or phrase on a vowel.
4. Watch What Just Happened and Current Quality.
5. Record Take 1, make one musical adjustment, then record Take 2.

If the app says the signal is too quiet, noisy, clipped, or low confidence, fix
the input before trusting the metrics. Move closer, sing a steadier vowel,
reduce background noise, or lower the input level if clipping appears.

Use Lock & Ring to compare changes. Do not chase every frame or percentage.
Room sound, microphone placement, vowel shape, and singer balance all affect
the readings.
